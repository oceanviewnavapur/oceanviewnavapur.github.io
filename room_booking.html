Description

Experience views of the Arabian sea and fall asleep looking at the moon-lit waves of the ocean. With contemporary and sleek designs inspired by the ocean, the rooms offers comfort with an ocean view from the windows, including a bathroom with shower bath. With a king size bed, our rooms are ideal for a couple's retreat or family holiday. After a fun-filled day at the resort, relax with our room service menu.
