Other stuff you may consider

Choose us if what you're looking for is to simply relax and chill with your favourite people!

  • Take a stroll on the beach

    In the evening the sun peeks up over the waves, and the thin rays glisten over the sparkling undulations of the ocean. The sunset over the ocean is truly one of the most beautiful wonders of nature.

  • Discover local cuisine

    Begin your foodie escape with a taste of the ultimate home-made Maharashtrian style "GAVTHI KOMBDI RASSA" (ORGANIC CHICKEN CURRY). The cuisine of Maharashtra spans from mild to spicy, and this curry is made with the trademark coconut milk used in lots of Maharashtrian cooking.

  • Take a ferry to Arnala island fort

    Arnala Fort is built on a small island of the port town of Arnala, located 15mins away from our property. The Portuguese controlled the island fort for two centuries; they used it to control shipping and navigation along the northern Konkan coast.

  • Farm activities

    A little ahead of the beach house we have our own farms fields, from green vegetables to flowers like Marigold & Sunflower. Explore the scenic beauty of the farm as you stroll around.
