Description

Indulge in luxury with our Premium and Deluxe category rooms facing the Arabian Sea. Enjoy panoramic views, plush bedding, and modern amenities in the Premium rooms along with a sea facing private balcony, while the Deluxe rooms offer spectacular sea views, luxurious furnishings, and personalized service.
